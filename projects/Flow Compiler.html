Introduction

Flow compiler was originally a solution designed solely for Particle Engine. The goal was to integrate a small scripting language directly into the engine to allow for custom behaviours to be implemented. However, it eventually evolved into a much more complex project, and has now been separated into its own solution.

Research and Requirements

  • Parse a C-like grammar
  • Compile into bytecode
  • Provide compile-time reflection information
  • Allow linking to external libraries

Ideation

The compiler is built as a recursive descent parser - they are one of the easiest compilers to implement, and conveniently allow a more accurate error reporting than other methods.
