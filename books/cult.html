Cult

As a child, I was always told to not question the book. It held the answers to every question, and was both my sword and shield against the wider world.

When my questions started to build is when the trouble seemed to start. Attempting to escape the followers that I was once a part of, I leave town, trying to build up a new reputation in a neighbouring city.

A bitter story that follows the steps of Johann - a 23 year old herder who lives on the outskirts of some rural town - as he escapes, is found, and eventually abducted from his home by the people he once was a part of.
