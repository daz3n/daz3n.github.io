The Pitch

Qi

Brief

QI Digital is a global Fintech company based in London. They provide an integrated solution for businesses, developers and payment service providers to accept and offer digital currencies as a payment method.

My task is to create a realistic go-to-market/business plan and present it in the form of a pitch deck, based upon how Web3 will open decentralised technologies, which are proving to democratise the status-quo for digital currencies.

Background

QI Digital is heavily invested in cryptocurrencies, and the digital market. Their business strategy relies on the success of the digital currencies that they offer solutions for.

Web3 is the latest iteration in the World Wide Web, based on blockchain technology. Since it’s based on blockchain technology, Web 3 will provide decentralisation, data security and privacy in a way similar to the promise of cryptocurrencies today.

Analysis

Creating a pitch deck for a startup like QI Digital will require a strong focus on the attraction of the underlying technologies to consumers. With more consumers becoming invested in cryptocurrencies than ever before, the expectation to easily transfer and spend it like a regular currency will grow, and QI Digital aims to fill that space.

By providing a method for PSP’s like Stripe and PayPal to interact with virtual currencies, exposure and interaction with these currencies is encouraged, providing a launchboard for its’ own development and outreach.
