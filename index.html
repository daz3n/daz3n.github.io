About Me

I'm a UK based software engineer, skilled in multiple frameworks and tech stacks.

I hold a passion for graphical programming, and have spent the last 4 years writing a game engine to recreate the high fidelity graphics seen in triple A games today. It's difficult, but the journey has been worth it - I don't ever expect to commercialise the engine, but it's still one of the largest projects I've ever attempted.

Although this website is supposed to be dedicated to the programming achievements of yours truly, I do actually have other hobbies. Having run competitively for a long time, I clearly have a taste for pain and suffering, and will do anything to sweat just that little bit more. I've won absolutely no medals, but I still enjoy the sport, so I soldier on regardless. Apart from running, I've earned qualifications in sailing, scuba diving, archery, kayaking, and skydiving. Although I've never done it, I would love to try base jumping just once, especially while I have the time.
