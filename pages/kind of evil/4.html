Kind of evil

Registered Magics

Fireball

Ignite the magic in your hands, hurling an incandescent ball of pure fire.

Bubble Shield

Create a spherical force shield around your body to repel projectiles, rain, and magic.

Unregistered Magics

Call Fire

Any material you touch with intent will become more flammable. Many rangers, hunters and trappers are born with the ability to do this. It manifests as a general skill at lighting fires.

Flame Whisper

Used by the protagonist to guide flames.

Spirit Commune

Share your mind with another creature. Often used to calm or communicate with animals.

Ghost Walk

Separate your spirit from your body, using magic to retain a connection between them. If your tether is destroyed, your spirit will be separated from your body until you perish.

Bless

Dissolve a part of your magic into an object or person to strengthen it.

Soul Sacrifice

Dissolve a part of your soul into a person or creature. While you both survive, you can feel their emotions and general state of wellbeing. They also have access to an amount of your magic that’s proportional to how much of your soul you dissolved into them. If they die, you will not regain your soul. If you have no more of your original soul, you will die. If you die, the blessing will slowly fade, as if it’s a plant that has stopped recieving nutrients.

Sight

Predict what the future holds. This is a rare gift, and often manifests as a surety in their present actions, as well as a strategic mind.

Banned Magics

Consume Life

Drain the energy, health, magic, and soul of a creature in contact with you. You will not gain everything that the other creature loses. Instead, some of the resources are lost in the process, passing into the fourth dimension, where they dissapate slowly into the world.
