Overview

Particle Engine is a high performance game engine, written from the ground up to take advantage of modern hardware. It provides a simple interface for the developer to use the GPU in almost every task, from mathematical functions to asset loading. Asynchronous functions and preloading capabilities ensure that the engine runs with as few stutters as possible, all while rendering thousands of lights at realtime framerates. The state-of-the-art material system provides a broad spectrum of material customisation options, from transparency to emission.

Since its inception, this engine has had thousands of hours of work put into polishing every facet of its inner workings to provide a smooth and intuitive experience in the editor. And for those cases where we just haven’t got what you need, we support a plugin system where you can add your own tools on top of the existing engine.
